Official page for SenseMeter app, including app information.
# Privacy Policy - SenseMeter

SenseMeter is a free utility app for daily sensor data monitoring (speed, altitude, acceleration, steps, noise, compass, etc.).

# Privacy Policy

- This app does not collect or transmit any personal information.
- All sensor data is processed locally on your device.
- No login or account is required.
- No advertising or tracking is included.

# App Information

- Name: SenseMeter
- Version: 1.0
- Supported Languages: Chinese, English, French, Spanish, German, Japanese
